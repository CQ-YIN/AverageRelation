Unravel the Emotional Distances

Average Relation delves into a world where blockchain meets the complexities of human connections. This project is a groundbreaking endeavor that invites you to evaluate the emotional distances in your personal relationships using a unique and thought-provoking method.

Valuing Bonds with a Measure of Distance

Distance is the ONLY unit here. Each NFT artwork symbolizes different facets of human relationships. You are invited to assign a value to these relationships, not in terms of monetary worth, but as a measure of emotional distance. The valuation range is set from a minimum of 0.1 ETH to a maximum of 10 ETH, with only one decimal place allowed. The key lies in the interpretation: the lower the number, the closer and more intimate the relationship. To be clearer, valuing a relationship at 0.5 ETH suggests a closer bond, reflecting a lesser emotional distance.

A Collaborative Valuation Experience

This project isn't just about individual perceptions. Each NFT's value evolves collaboratively, shaped by the collective input of its owners. As you interact with these artworks, you're participating in a shared exploration of what each relationship means to different individuals.

Embark on a Journey of Reflection

Join us in this unique exploration where each NFT is a narrative about connections and distances. Reflect on the emotional proximity in your relationships, engage in a community of introspective collectors, and discover where you stand in the intricate web of human emotions.
